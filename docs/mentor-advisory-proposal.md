# Industry Advisor Partnership Proposal

**Squarerootz — AI Education Platform**

---

## Executive Summary

Squarerootz is building India's most job-market-aligned AI education platform. We design curricula that anticipate industry requirements 12 weeks ahead, ensuring our graduates are prepared for tomorrow's roles, not yesterday's job postings.

We are selectively inviting experienced AI/ML practitioners to join our **Industry Advisory Network** — a group of working professionals who help us maintain curriculum authenticity, deliver high-impact sessions, and ensure our students learn from those actively building production AI systems.

This is not a full-time commitment. We're looking for practitioners who want to contribute to AI education while continuing their primary work, with flexible engagement and fair compensation.

---

## Why We're Reaching Out to You

Your experience in **AI/ML** aligns with our mission to deliver production-relevant education. We've reviewed your work in **AI/ML** and believe your perspective would be valuable to our students and curriculum.

We're not looking for course creators or full-time instructors. We're looking for practitioners who are actively working in the field and can bring real-world authenticity to our programs.

---

## Partnership Structure

We offer three tiers of involvement. You can choose one or combine based on your availability and interest.

### Tier 1: Curriculum Advisor
**Commitment:** 4-6 hours per quarter
**Compensation:** ₹15,000 - ₹25,000 per review cycle

**Responsibilities:**
- Review course curriculum for technical accuracy and industry relevance
- Identify gaps between our content and current job market requirements
- Suggest updates based on emerging tools, frameworks, and practices
- Provide written feedback on syllabus structure and project assignments

**What You Get:**
- Listed as Industry Advisor on course page and marketing materials
- Early access to all Squarerootz courses
- Network access to other advisors in the program

---

### Tier 2: Guest Expert
**Commitment:** 2-4 sessions per quarter (1.5-2 hours each)
**Compensation:** ₹8,000 - ₹15,000 per session

**Responsibilities:**
- Deliver live webinars or masterclasses on specialized topics
- Participate in student Q&A sessions (Ask Me Anything format)
- Share case studies from your professional experience
- Optional: Review student capstone projects (additional compensation)

**Session Formats:**
- **Masterclass (90 min):** Deep dive into a specific topic
- **AMA Session (60 min):** Open Q&A with students
- **Case Study (60 min):** Walk through a real project you've worked on
- **Career Session (45 min):** Interview prep, resume review, career guidance

**What You Get:**
- All Tier 1 benefits
- Session recordings for your personal branding (with mutual consent)
- LinkedIn/social media promotion of your sessions
- Priority consideration for paid consulting opportunities with our corporate partners

---

### Tier 3: Lead Advisor
**Commitment:** Ongoing relationship (8-12 hours per month)
**Compensation:** ₹40,000 - ₹60,000 monthly retainer

**Responsibilities:**
- Serve as primary advisor for 1-2 courses
- Monthly curriculum review and updates
- At least 2 live sessions per month
- Available for ad-hoc consultations (within reasonable limits)
- Participate in quarterly advisory board meetings
- Help interview and onboard new advisors in your domain

**What You Get:**
- All Tier 1 and Tier 2 benefits
- Revenue share on courses you advise (negotiable)
- Speaking opportunities at Squarerootz events and partner conferences
- First right of refusal for full-time/consulting roles if we scale

## Legal Framework

The formal agreement will cover:

1. **Scope of Work:** Specific deliverables and expectations
2. **Compensation:** Rates, payment terms, invoicing process
3. **Intellectual Property:** Your contributions become Squarerootz property; you retain rights to your pre-existing materials and general expertise
4. **Confidentiality:** NDA covering student data, business information, and proprietary curriculum
5. **Non-Exclusivity:** You're free to work with other education platforms (no exclusivity required)
6. **Liability:** Limited liability for both parties
7. **Termination:** 30-day notice from either party, immediate termination for cause
8. **Conflict of Interest:** Disclosure requirement if your employer competes directly

*We'll share the full contract for review before any commitment.*

## About Squarerootz

Squarerootz is an AI education platform focused on practical, job-market-aligned training. Our philosophy:

- **Concepts over tools:** We teach the "why" so students can adapt when tools change
- **12-week-ahead curriculum:** We anticipate where the job market is heading, not where it was
- **Open-source first:** We minimize paid dependencies, making our courses accessible
- **Production focus:** Every course includes real-world projects, not toy examples

We're a growing team passionate about making AI education authentic and effective.
